Short Biography

My name is Evodie, I am from the Democratic Republic of Congo, and I have been living in the U.S for almost seven years. Growing up, I had a big interest for fashion and communication, so when I came here, I wanted to major in communication. I then was introduced to the “Digital Marketing” degree by a friend, which was related to communication.

Academic Goals

When it comes to my academic journey, my goal was and still is gaining as many skills and knowledge as possible that will be beneficial for not only my career but in life too. Majoring in digital marketing helped gain marketing, communication, technology, and web development skills.

Career Summary:

I have been working as an intern then a full-time employee with responsibilities of planning, building, and implementing digital marketing campaigns across multiple channels and platforms, administer the company’s website and publish content to support their marketing campaigns.
